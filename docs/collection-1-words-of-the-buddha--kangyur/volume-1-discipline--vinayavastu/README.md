# <div align="center">Discipline</br>(Vinayavastu)</div>
Homage to the [Three Jewels](../../../GLOSSARY.md#three-jewels).  

Homage to them who severed the bonds,  
Destroyed the whole host of [tīrthikas](../../../GLOSSARY.md#tīrthikas),  
Vanquished the armies of [Māra](../../../GLOSSARY.md#māra),  
And thus discovered this awakening.  

To begin with, it is hard to renounce a householder’s concerns and [go forth](../../../GLOSSARY.md#go-forth).  
Having gone forth, it is hard to find happiness in wandering.  
It is hard to cheerfully fulfill a [pledge](../../../GLOSSARY.md#pledge).  
It is hard to fail as a well-practiced wearer of [ochre robes](../../../GLOSSARY.md#ochre-robes).
