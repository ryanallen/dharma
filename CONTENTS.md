# Teachings</br>(Dharma)
Three vehicles to guide your way.

## Key
1. `Vin.` = Vinaya Piṭaka / monastic discipline books
1. `DN`, `MN`, `SN`, `AN`, `KN` = the five main Nikāya collections in the Pāli Canon: `DN` = Dīgha Nikāya (`D` = Dīgha, long), `MN` = Majjhima Nikāya (`M` = Majjhima, middle-length), `SN` = Saṃyutta Nikāya (`S` = Saṃyutta, connected), `AN` = Aṅguttara Nikāya (`A` = Aṅguttara, numbered), `KN` = Khuddaka Nikāya (`K` = Khuddaka, minor)
1. `Kp`, `Dhp`, `Ud`, `Iti`, `Snp`, `Vv`, `Pv`, `Thag`, `Thig`, `Ap`, `Bv`, `Cp`, `Ja`, `Nd`, `Ps` = standard short titles for individual Pāli works: `Kp` = Khuddakapāṭha, `Dhp` = Dhammapada, `Ud` = Udāna, `Iti` = Itivuttaka, `Snp` = Sutta Nipāta, `Vv` = Vimānavatthu, `Pv` = Petavatthu, `Thag` = Theragāthā, `Thig` = Therīgāthā, `Ap` = Apadāna, `Bv` = Buddhavaṃsa, `Cp` = Cariyāpiṭaka, `Ja` = Jātaka, `Nd` = Niddesa, `Ps` = Paṭisambhidāmagga
1. `Dhs`, `Vibh`, `Dhātuk`, `Pug`, `Kv`, `Yam`, `Paṭṭhāna` = standard short titles for Abhidhamma works: `Dhs` = Dhammasaṅgaṇī, `Vibh` = Vibhaṅga, `Dhātuk` = Dhātukathā, `Pug` = Puggalapaññatti, `Kv` = Kathāvatthu, `Yam` = Yamaka, `Paṭṭhāna` = Paṭṭhāna
1. `T` = Taishō canon number in the Chinese Buddhist canon
1. `Toh.` = Tōhoku catalog number in the Tibetan canon
1. `I-V`, `1-34`, `45-93`, etc. = volume, fascicle, chapter, or text ranges within a collection
1. `Assembly` = an internal division inside a larger anthology such as the Mahāratnakūṭa
1. `Sūtra`, `Nikāya`, `Āgama`, `Piṭaka`, `Śāstra` = discourse, collection, parallel collection, basket, and treatise/commentary
1. `84k` = Source: 84000: Translating the Words of the Buddha. License: CC BY-NC-ND 4.0. Link: https://84000.co/
